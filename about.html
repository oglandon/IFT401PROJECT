About Us.

At Proton Trading, we are redefining the stock trading experience with cutting-edge technology and an unwavering commitment to security, speed, and efficiency. Our platform is built on a robust foundation of FastAPI, PostgreSQL, and Amazon AWS, ensuring seamless performance, real-time data processing, and rock-solid reliability.

We believe that stock trading should be fast, intuitive, and secure. Our mission is to empower traders with a platform that not only meets but exceeds modern trading standards—delivering rapid execution speeds, real-time insights, and a streamlined, visually appealing interface.

Proton Trading harnesses the power of FastAPI for lightning-fast backend processing, coupled with the reliability of PostgreSQL for secure and efficient data management. Leveraging Amazon AWS, we ensure high availability, scalability, and top-tier security for all your trading activities.

Blazing Fast Execution: Execute trades with minimal latency, giving you the edge in fast-moving markets.
Robust Security: Your data and transactions are protected with industry-leading encryption and security protocols.
User-Centric Design: Experience a sleek, modern interface designed for both efficiency and aesthetic appeal, making trading not just effective but enjoyable.
Cloud-Powered Reliability: AWS infrastructure guarantees stability, uptime, and effortless scaling as your trading needs grow.

At Proton Trading, we’re not just another trading platform—we’re shaping the future of trading. Whether you’re a seasoned investor or just starting your journey, Proton Trading delivers the speed, security, and reliability you need to stay ahead of the market.
